1. **User Journey:** 
   - Hero: Gets attention â†’ "What is this?"
   - Video: Shows product â†’ "How does it work?"

   - Visual proof before commitment
   - Builds trust early
   - Reduces bounce rate

4. **Design Flow:**
   - Breaks up text-heavy sections
   - Adds visual variety
   - Creates natural rhythm
